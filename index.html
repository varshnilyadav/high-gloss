Core Protection Services
01

Ceramic Coating

High-end paint protection providing a long-lasting, hydrophobic shield and a brilliant high-gloss finish that repels water and contaminants.

02

Paint Protection Film

Clear, durable PPF (Clear Bra) engineered to absorb impacts from rock chips, scratches, and road debris — invisible armour for your paint.

03

Window Tinting

High-quality films that block UV rays, reduce cabin heat, and enhance privacy — applied with precision for a flawless, bubble-free finish.

04

Anti-Corrosion Treatment

Specialized underbody coatings that form a permanent barrier against rust, salt, and environmental damage — protecting your vehicle from the ground up.

Detailing & Restoration
05

Exterior Detailing

Meticulous paint restoration, multi-stage machine polishing, and buffing to eliminate swirl marks and revive a mirror-perfect showroom shine.

06

Interior Deep Cleaning

Thorough cleaning and conditioning of seats, carpets, dashboard, and upholstery — every surface treated and restored to pristine condition.

07

Steam & Premium Wash

Customization & Enhancements
